Quartermaster is a self-hosted kitchen inventory app for households that want a clear record of food and supplies.

Track products, quantities, units, batches, storage locations, expiry dates, and stock history. Add items manually or scan barcodes, organize your pantry, fridge, and freezer, and keep a durable record of adds, uses, corrections, discards, and restores.

Quartermaster is built for people who want ownership of their household data. Run your own server, connect the iOS app, invite household members, and keep inventory records where you choose.
